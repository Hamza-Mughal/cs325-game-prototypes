Protect Or Die

  • High Concept: Protect the package by surviving!
  • Theme: Person who needs protection
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Keyboard Randomness: Deterministic play style: Achiever
  • Player Experience Goals: Player should experience accomplishment
  • Gameplay: Player can hit spacebar, and the arrow keys. Core mechanic is to use arrow key to select the correct answer. If player chooses wrong answer, it deducts from the timer. If timer hits 0, the player loses.
  • Prototype Goal: Prototyping the difficulty of questions/use of game states/arrow keys
  • Features: Player is trying to safely deliver the package by answering math questions in a timely manner
  • Setting/Premise: Set in 1940s New York where a package needs to be delivered. However the delivery man is stopped by mathematical questions.
  • Story: A package needs to be delivered to a destination - it will determine the fate of Earth itself!
  • Target Audience: Trying to reach high school students.
  • Play Time: About 1 minute.
  • Strategies: Choosing correct answer and knowing simple Calculus will help.
