Life or Death

  • High Concept: Stop the Evil Doctor and rescue your organs!
  • Theme: Organ Donor
  • Mandated Variety: Input used: Direct Randomness: A lot of randomness Genre: Shooter Play style: Achiever
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should experience excitement and accomplishment.
  • Gameplay: Up Arrow: Move Up Down Arrow: Move Down Right Arrow: Shoot The player can shoot at any time and any moment However, the player must be cautious of the enemys attacks If an enemy projectile hits the player, the player will lose
  • Prototype Goal: Prototype is evaluating random AI movement/shooting to determine best RNG that is moderately difficult for the player
  • Features: Although the player can only move up and down, he/she must make his decision carefully. One wrong move can result in death! Enemy projectiles randomly fly across attempting to kill the player. The enemy is also randomly flying around, with the player trying to kill him.
  • Setting/Premise: The game is set in a hospital in Koen Kaen, Thailand. Koen Kaen is a popular attraction for medical tourism, however this attracts the wrong crowd of people. The characters are Nabil the patient and the Evil Doctor Vargas. This game world is interesting because it is in a crowded city with great wonders, however making the right decision is a must.
  • Story: In the United States, Nabil needed surgery to replace his ruptured Jejunum however there was no donor available in the United States. Nabil searched for months for a donor to no avail until one day he received a message. The message stated that there was someone willing to donate their organ, however Nabil would have to travel to Koen Kaen, Thailand. Upon arrival to Koen Kaen, Nabil met up with Daniel and they both went to the hospital to start the organ transfer. As Nabil was sedated and asleep, Vargas stole Nabils organs. Nabil awakes, just in time as Vargas begins to escapes, to recover his organs.
  • Target Audience: Target audience is people between the age of 15 - 20.
  • Play Time: Considering the game is somewhat RNG based, gameplay can be between 1 to 3 minutes.
  • Strategies: Dominant strategy will be to determine the move of the enemy to shoot at the correct moment. Being able to dodge projectiles is also a needed skill.
