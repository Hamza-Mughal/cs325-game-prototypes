Jump, Jump, Jump!

  • High Concept: Avoid dieing to retrieve your precious items.
  • Theme: Snob and Phone calls aren't returned
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Mouse primarily, with keyboard to restart game. Randomness: deterministic. Play styles: Performed, Achiever, Competitor. Genres: Adventure, platformer.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should feel a rush of accomplishment as they successfully avoid obstacles to win.
  • Gameplay:
  • Mouse: Make player move upwards.
  • W: Restart game at end.
  • Game automatically restarts if out of bounds or collide with obstacle.
  • Player earns points touching the necklaces.
  • Player needs minimum of 500 points to win the game.
  • Touch the power up to gain extra points, but be aware of your surrounding!
  • Prototype Goal: Using mouse to move player up, also determining if obstacles are set up correctly and at correct speeds.
  • Features: Obstacles move at random speeds, and spawn at seperate times. Auditory cue given when phone obstacles approach. Power up to gain extra points.
  • Setting/Premise: Set in the medieval ages where gorillas and humans ruled together. Nabil is a high class elite member (a snob), while the gorilla is a low peasent. The gorilla wants to get in touch with Nabil, however he wont returns his calls. So the only thing he can do is kill Nabil!
  • Story: Nabil is a member of the elite. He spends most of his days thinking about how rich he is and touches his necklaces non stop. A gorilla appeared at his doorstep one day to ask him for his money. As soon as Nabil saw the gorilla, he laughed and said he does not talk to peasants. The gorilla was shocked and left, and then began to call Nabil's home. Nabil did not pick up which enraged the gorilla. The gorilla learned psychic powers and transported Nabil into an evil world where the gorilla tries to kill Nabil.
  • Target Audience: Targeted towards audiences who enjoy platforming games that can be a challenge to beat.
  • Play Time: Depending on RNG, gameplay can take up to 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  • Strategies: Pre determine when and where next obstacle will spawn so you can move appropriately to avoid them.
