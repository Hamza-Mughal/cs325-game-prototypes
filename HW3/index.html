  • High Concept: Survive while going through deadly rings of fire.
  • Theme: Person who will do whatever it takes
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Mouse. Randomness: Determinisitic and random. Play style: Performer/Achiever
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should feel accomplishment as they pass each ring of fire.
  • Gameplay: Actions player can perform is moving upwards by mouse clicking. The system dynamic is that the hole spawns randomly, so it is up to the player to be smart about their movement. The core mechanic of the game is to get through the ring of fire alive. Inputs: Mouse Click to move upwards. Do not mouse click to go downwards.
  • Prototype Goal: Game mechanic being evaluated is whether player speed is good and if ring of fires are too far apart or too tight for hard/easy passage.
  • Features: Player has to have good movement to pass through otherwise he/she will restart. Ring of fires are psuedo-random.
  • Setting/Premise: Game is set in the Krakatoa Volcano in Indonesia. The character is a magician named Nabil. This game world is unique because it involves traveling across multiple different countries to try to rescue what has been stolen. The occupent (Nabil) is trying to save his lucky playing cards that were stolen from him by Keith the Madman. As close as Nabil gets to saving his cards, there ends up being a new twist....
  • Story: Nabil is a street magician who was showing off what he learned over the years. Every day was a good day in the sense that he made a lot of people happy. One day, a mysterious figure showed up to see what the ruccus was about. When Nabil showed his trick, this mysterious man was astonished. This man wanted the cards for himself so he could possess the magic himself. The man distracted Nabil and as soon as Nabil looked away, his cards were gone. Nabil knew who took his cards, however the hard part would be getting them back. Nabil searched for days for any clues about the whereabouts of this man until he got a lucky break. He was informed in a tavern in Kathmandu that the man he was looking for was in Indonesia. Off Nabil went to try to rescue his magic back from this mysterious man.
