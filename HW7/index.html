Escape the Police

  • High Concept: Escape the police
  • Theme: Car accident
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Keyboard and Mouse Randomness: Non-Deterministic play style: Achiever
  • Player Experience Goals: Player should experience accomplishment
  • Gameplay: Player hits the up and down arrows to start game. Player then uses mouse clicks to move the car left or right.
  • Prototype Goal: Prototyping the randomness of car spawns and fluidity of car movement
  • Features: Player is trying to out speed the police in a high chase down the road
  • Setting/Premise: Set in Palm Beach Florida, Nabil is known for his habit of street racing until one day the Police had enough.
  • Story: Trying to earn money for street racing, Nabil must out manueavur the police otherwise he will not be able to feed his family.
  • Target Audience: Trying to reach people 12 and up.
  • Play Time: About 2 to 3 minutes.
  • Strategies: Moving left or right at the correct moment will help in not being captured by police.
